About

The Scottish Remote Sensing Portal has been developed as a partnership between Scottish Government and Joint Nature Conservation Committee (JNCC).

The portal currently contains Scottish Public Sector LiDAR data which you can download by 10km tiles and via WMS web services.

For more information on the LiDAR data, please contact us. For more information on a particular dataset, please use the contact information provided in the full metadata.

All data is made available under the Open Government Licence v3 unless otherwise stated.
